Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller about a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform "inception" — planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe. Each actor delivers a powerful performance that adds depth to the film's complex narrative.
